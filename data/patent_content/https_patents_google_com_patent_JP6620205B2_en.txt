The disclosed systems and methods apply to media content such as audio and / or video content, images, and / or any other content that can be collected and / or disseminated, such as via a media sharing service or website can do. More specifically, many social networking services allow users to create links and / or embedded media players that can be embedded within social networking services (eg, within a web page or application associated with a particular social network). Through which media items (such as streaming video stored in a media sharing service) can be “shared” or disseminated. Such sharing of media items can increase the engagement of the user with the social network, both for the media sharing service (by increasing the audience rating of the media items provided by the media sharing service) and for the social network. May be advantageous). However, while media sharing services may include additional media items that may be of interest to viewers of media content, various constraints and restrictions associated with the presentation of embedded media items in social networks (e.g., Constraints) can make it more difficult for viewers to find such additional content.

As described herein, upon receiving a request for a media item and determining that such a request originated from a social network (e.g., in connection with an embedded media item in a post on the social network) Can identify one or more additional media items stored in the media sharing service, and links to such media items in the social network in conjunction with the originally requested media item Can be provided.
