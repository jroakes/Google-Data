Abstract
Statistical data relating to one or more parameters associated with an advertisement may be gathered. The statistical data may be filtered to a Universal Resource Locator (URL) or domain level. The statistical data may be aggregated and evaluated, including applying a filter to the statistical data. The filtered data may be delivered to an advertiser. The advertiser may receive the filtered data in a report and modify their advertising campaign in accordance with the report.
Description
Technical field
This instructions relates generally to online advertisement.
Background technology
In online advertisement, some advertisers can advertise on a plurality of websites, are advertising all over the world, and are having many dissimilar advertisements (for example text, video).The advertiser wishes to have the statistical report that useful information is provided, make them can adjust their advertising campaign, for example, reduce the area decreases advertisement of advertising on the website of expected result or never producing expected result, change advertisement (" ad ") type or the like not producing.In order to eliminate the website that does not have effect, the advertiser need have the report of the positional information that provides relevant with the position of the advertisement that shows them, described positional information such as resource locator (URL)/domain information.Simultaneously, large-scale online advertisement can produce a large amount of raw data.Collect such data on a large scale and generate report and may expend a lot of times and computational resource.
