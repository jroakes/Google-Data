Fundamentos da Invenção [002] Sistemas de comunicação de rádio geralmente proporcionam comunicação de voz e de dados de duas vias entre localizações remotas. Examples of such systems are cellular and personal communication system (PCS) radio systems, trunk radio systems, dispatch radio networks and mobile, global, personal communication systems (GMPCS) such as satellite-based systems . Mobile stations, also known as telephone, portable or radiotelephone devices, conform to the system standard for communicating with one or more fixed base stations.
