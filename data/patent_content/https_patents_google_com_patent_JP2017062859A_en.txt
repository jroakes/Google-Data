(Overview)
To facilitate image searching and sharing, users can organize digital media aggregates into different folders by album name or date. A user can also associate a tag or tag word with an image or group of images. The tag includes one or more keywords that describe the content of the image. However, searching for images associated with a specific event can be a time intensive task when only a limited amount of information is known by the user. For example, a user may desire to attend an event and view images taken at that event. In such a scenario, the user may know only the names of one or two individuals attending the event, along with the location of the event. If the database has a significant amount of images, the user will manually examine each image or album and retrieve images from the event.
加えて、メディア共有サイトは、概して、具体的イベントに関連する画像を自動的に検索する能力を提供しない。例えば、ユーザが、あるイベントに出席し、イベントに出席した数人の他の個人を知っている場合があるが、しかしながら、そのイベントから写真を読み出す自動方法は存在しない。概して、ユーザは、イベントで画像を撮影した別の個人を知っており、画像に関してその個人のアルバムを検索する必要があるであろう。 In addition, media sharing sites generally do not provide the ability to automatically search for images associated with specific events. For example, a user may attend an event and know several other individuals who have attended the event, however, there is no automatic way to retrieve photos from that event. In general, the user will know another individual who took the image at the event and will need to search that individual's album for the image.
