Abstract
Systems, methods, and media for managing an entertainment system are provided. In some implementations, systems for managing an entertainment system are provided, the systems comprising: at least one hardware processor configured to: detect a first instruction: select a component of the entertainment system; determine a first state of the component; store an indication of the first state; detect a second instruction; retrieve the indication of the first state; generate a third instruction based on the indication of the first state; and transmit the third instruction to the component of the entertainment system.

Background technology
Entertainment systems can be used to watch TV, listens to the music or consume the media content of other suitable type any.Some entertainment systems can comprise source component and render component.Such as, source component can be configured to render component to provide audio frequency and/or vision signal to carry out the tuner played up and various media player.As another example, render component can be television set, home theater receiver, amplifier and the miscellaneous equipment being configured to produce sound or video.
Switching between the different source components of entertainment systems may be troublesome sometimes.Such as, if user wants from seeing that radiovision is switched to viewing Blu-ray Disc, user may need to perform multiple order, such as: Blu-ray player is changed in the useful signal source of television set; Open Blu-ray player; And change the volume of television set according to the sound levels exported by Blu-ray player, make the sound when Blu-ray Disc starts to play both not too large also not too little.Afterwards, if user wants to continue to see TV, he or she may need again to perform multiple order of the effect putting upside down former order and make television set again show radiovision.But this order may need user manually to input via Long-distance Control, and this may be inconvenient for user.
Correspondingly, exist for the demand for the new system of managing entertainment system, method and medium.
