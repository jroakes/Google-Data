Abstract
The invention relates to a multi-community content sharing in an online social network. An online social network system (100) can be used for issuing content in the online social network. Products comprise codes for implementing a method, and the method starts from receiving of the content to be published to a main community. The invention also provides a tag (420) which is linked with the content. The tag (420) is used for identifying communities to which the main community publishes the content in the online social network. Codes of which the content is displayed on a web page of the main community during implementation and on web pages of all identified communities are generated. The content can comprise one event or a plurality of events, images, forums and topics.
