TV and radio broadcasts, mobile telephone, satellite radio and television, wireless computer networks, Bluetooth, global positioning services, police broadcast and many other general and specialized applications used everyday. It is usually difficult for these applications to use the same frequencies at the same time. In order to avoid such a disturbance, the radio spectrum is divided into different sections, each section being allocated to one or more services which, generally speaking, can co-exist.

Die DF-Antennen sind aus mehreren Gründen schwierig zu verwenden, einschließlich der Tatsache, dass die Richtung, aus der ein Signal an einen bestimmten Ort ankommt, nicht immer den Ort des Senders angibt, aber nicht eingeschränkt auf die Tatsache. The method includes terminating by the computing hardware the transmission operations of all transmitters in the network during a threshold period. If the fault assessment indicates the persistence of the interference between the two or more transmitters in the network, the method includes determining by the computing hardware that the interfering transmitter is within the network; and performing, by the data processing hardware, a fault isolation routine configured to identify the interfering transmitter.

Das Verfahren enthält das Beenden der Sendeoperationen aller Sender in dem Netz während eines Schwellenzeitraums. Das Verfahren enthält das Anfordern einer Störungsbewertung 206 des Netzes für den Schwellenzeitraum, wenn alle Sender in dem Netz die Sendeoperationen beendet haben. Wenn die Störungsbewertung die Beendigung der Störung zwischen den Sendern in dem Netz angibt, enthält das Verfahren das Bestimmen, dass sich ein störender Sender außerhalb des Netzes befindet. Wenn die Störungsbewertung die Fortdauer der Störung zwischen den Sendern in dem Netz angibt, enthält das Verfahren das Bestimmen, dass sich der störende Sender innerhalb des Netzes befindet; und das Ausführen einer Störungsisolationsroutine 230, die konfiguriert ist, um den störenden Sender zu identifizieren.According to one embodiment, a system is provided for identifying an interfering transmitter 110 in a network 10 . 10a . 10b which is configured to perform operations including receiving a fault indication 204 that indicates interference between the transmitters in the network. The method includes stopping transmission operations of all transmitters in the network during a threshold period. The method includes requesting a fault assessment 206 the network for the threshold period when all transmitters in the network have completed the transmission operations. If the interference rating indicates the termination of the interference between the transmitters in the network, the method includes determining that an interfering transmitter is outside the network. If the interference rating indicates the persistence of the interference between the transmitters in the network, the method includes determining that the interfering transmitter is within the network; and performing a fault isolation routine 230 , which is configured to identify the interfering transmitter.
