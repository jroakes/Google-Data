DETAILED DESCRIPTION Publishers are increasing the amount of graphic novel content available digitally. There is also a comprehensive print collection of graphic novels, comic books, and comic strips dating back to the 19th century. One historian even argues that works of art made by ancient civilizations, such as the Roman Trajan column and the Bayou tapestry, are essentially the same form of art. For convenience, the term graphic novel is used herein to refer to any such content that comprises a series of ordered images that are narrative.
グラフィックノベルを読むことは、テキストベースの書籍を読むこととは異なる。現地に特有の読み順で（たとえば、英語圏の国では左から右へおよび上から下へ）読まれるテキストを主に通じて物語を語るよりもむしろ、グラフィックノベルの話は、（パネルとも称される）順序付けられた画像と吹き出しとの組合せを通して伝えられる。場合によっては、吹き出しは複数のパネルに重なる。さらに、ある事例（たとえば、多くの日本語のグラフィックノベル）では、テキストは右から左に読まれる。その結果、電子装置上に効果的にグラフィックノベルを表示することには特定の課題がある。すなわち、画面の大きさが異なる、テキストベースの書籍向けに開発されたナビゲーション技術はユーザがどのようにグラフィックノベルを読むかを反映しない、パネルおよび吹き出しが読まれる順序は左から右または上から下でないことがある、所与の画像の他の画像に対する文脈が重要であり得るなどである。 Reading graphic novels is different from reading text-based books. Rather than telling stories mainly through text that is read in a local reading order (for example, from left to right and top to bottom in English-speaking countries), the story of a graphic novel (also called a panel) Communicated through a combination of ordered images and speech balloons. In some cases, the balloon overlaps multiple panels. In addition, in some cases (for example, many Japanese graphic novels), text is read from right to left. As a result, there are specific challenges in displaying graphic novels effectively on electronic devices. That is, navigation technology developed for text-based books with different screen sizes does not reflect how users read graphic novels. The order in which panels and callouts are read is left to right or top to bottom. The context of a given image with respect to other images may be important, and so on.

Each node analyzes the input data it receives to determine whether the features it detects can be present in the input image. If it is determined that the feature exists, the node is activated. The activated node modifies the input data based on the weight of the activated node and sends the modified input data to one or more nodes in the next layer of the neural network. When a termination node in the neural network is activated, the neural network outputs a prediction that a feature corresponding to the termination node is present in the input image. In one embodiment, a percentage accuracy of the correctness of the prediction is assigned to the prediction based on the weight assigned to each node along the path taken through the neural network.
